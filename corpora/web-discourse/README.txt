= Data

=== gold.data.persuasive

 - 990 comments and forum posts labeled as persuasive or non-persuasive (annotation study 1)

=== gold.data.toulmin

 - 340 documents annotated with extended Toulming model (annotation study 2)

=== unlabeled.raw.corpus

 - Remaining 4334 documents from the raw corpus that have not been annotated in either of the studies
